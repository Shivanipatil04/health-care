Symptoms of covid-19

1.Fever: A high body temperature, usually above 38°C (100.4°F).

2.Cough: A dry cough that persists and may be accompanied by throat irritation.

3.Fatigue: Persistent tiredness or lack of energy.

4.Shortness of Breath: Difficulty breathing or feeling breathless, especially during physical activity.

5.Muscle or Body Aches: Generalized body discomfort or muscle pain.

6.Sore Throat: Irritation or pain in the throat.
