Precautions

  • Apply a cold or warm compress to your forehead or the back of your neck.
  • Practice deep breathing exercises or meditation to relax your body and mind.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated, as dehydration can contribute to headaches.
  • Gently massage your temples, neck, or scalp to help alleviate tension.
  • Use a headache balm or essential oils such as peppermint or lavender, applying them to your temples or forehead.
  • Avoid triggers such as bright lights, loud noises, strong smells, or certain foods if you know they typically cause headaches.
  • Get regular exercise and maintain a healthy lifestyle, as physical activity and a balanced diet can help prevent headaches.
